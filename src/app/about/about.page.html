About

DutchMate is a flashcards app whose main purpose is to help people memorizing Dutch words.

DutchMate has 2 main functionalities:

  1. Notebook
  2. Practice

In the "Notebook", users can add all the new words they have learned. Words are categorized in 6 categories:

  1. Noun
  2. Verb
  3. Adjective
  4. Adverb
  5. Proposition
  6. Conjunction

This categorization is meant to enhance the user experience when adding new words

In the "Practice", users can practice the words they have added using the flashcards technique.
